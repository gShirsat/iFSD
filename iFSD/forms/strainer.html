ZeeWeed systems require prescreens in order to remove solids that can damage membrane fibers if allowed into the membrane train. Providing and maintaining proper screening is required for the purpose of warranty. Prescreening requirements for ZW systems depend on application and membrane product.
All pre screens must be mesh, punched hole or disc filter type with a stated opening size in all dimensions at or below the value specified by the tool. Wedge wire material or slot opening is not acceptable.
Selected screen should not have areas where internal bypassing is possible or a design that is susceptible to bypass.
Guidelines for prescreening for non MBR ZW membrane products and applications
• All ZeeWeed ultrafiltration membranes and applications require the use of a 500 micron or less screen size (100 micron for SeaWater applications). Guidelines for prescreening for MBR ZW membrane products and applications
• Minimum screening requirement is less than 200 micron mesh or punched hole screens
• Strongly recommended t is less than 100 micron mesh or punched hole screens
