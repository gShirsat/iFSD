Calculations

Number of streams required to execute the unitop
Input- 1 Stream
Output - 1 Stream


Flux = Flow Rate / (pi/4*(Diameter/12)^2) (gpm/ft2)
Based on Temperature select Density, Viscosity, Diffusion Coefficient O2, Diffusion Coefficient N2,H O2, H N2, PH2O
Mass Velocity = (((Flow Rate/4.4029)/3600)*Density) / (pi/4*(Diameter*0.0254)^2) (kg/m2-s)
Packing Height = ((Tower Packing Depth Stage 1 + Tower Packing Depth Stage 2)/2)*0.3048 Average height per stage (m)
HTU O2 = ((Mass Velocity / Viscosity)^0.22)*((Viscosity/(Diffusion Coefficient O2*1000))^0.5)/430 (m)
HTU N2 = ((Mass Velocity / Viscosity)^0.22)*((Viscosity/(Diffusion Coefficient N2*1000))^0.5)/430 (m)
B O2 = Packing Height / (HTU O2 * 2.30259) non-dimensional unit
related to packing depth
B N2 = Packing Height / (HTU N2 * 2.30259) non-dimensional unit
related to packing depth

Stage 1

Outlet O2 Stage 1 = (Inlet O2*Outlet O2 Stage2)^0.5 (ppm)
Xe1 = ((10^B O2)* Outlet O2 Stage 1 - Inlet O2 )/((10^B O2)-1) non-dimensional unit
related to vacuum tower
packing depth and inlet/outlet CO2 levels
Outlet N2 Stage 1 = Inlet N2*(8*(HO2*Xe1/HN2)*((10^BN2)-1)+7*(Inlet O2 - Outlet O2
Stage1))/(8*(HO2*Xe1/HN2)*((10^BN2)-1)+7*(10^BN2)*(Inlet O2 - Outlet O2 Stage1))		 (ppm)
O2 Flow Rate = (Flow Rate/4.4029)*(Inlet O2 - Outlet O2 Stage 1 )/32 (mol/hr)
N2 Flow Rate = (Flow Rate/4.4029)*(Inlet N2 - Outlet N2 Stage 1 )/28 (mol/hr)
CO2 Flow Rate = (Flow Rate/4.4029)*(Inlet CO2 -(Inlet CO2^0.5))/44 (mol/hr)
O2 Moles = O2 Flow Rate / (O2 Flow Rate + N2 Flow Rate + CO2 Flow Rate)
Pressure Air = H O2 * Xe1/ O2 Moles (mm Hg) pressure (a function of
vacuum tower packing depth)
Pressure Total = Pressure H2O + Pressure Air (mm Hg) pressure (a function of
vacuum tower packing depth)
Gas mass flow rate air = Flow Rate O2 + Flow Rate N2 + Flow Rate CO2 (mol/hr) quantity of gas (PV value), a mass flow rate
Gas mass flow rate H2O = Pressure H2O* Gas mass flow rate air / Pressure Air (mol/hr)
Gas mass flow rate air infiltrating the system = 2.1 (mol/hr)
Gas mass flow rate H2O infiltrating the system = Pressure H2O* Gas mass flow rate air infiltrating the system / Pressure Air (mol/hr)
Volume Flow Rate Total = 0.3097*(Gas mass flow rate air + Gas mass flow rate H2O + Gas mass flow rate air infiltrating the system + Gas mass flow rate H2O infiltrating the system) / Pressure Total (m3/min)
Non-Condensable Gas Load = (29/454) *(Gas mass flow rate air + Gas mass flow rate air infiltrating the system) (lb/hr)
H2O Load = (18/454)*(Gas mass flow rate H2O + Gas mass flow rate H2O infiltrating the system) (lb/hr)
Flow = Volume Flow Rate Total*35.314/0.85 (acfm)
Vacuum = 0.95*Pressure Total (mm Hg)

Stage 2

Xe2 = ((10^B O2)*Outlet O2 Stage 2 - Outlet O2 Stage 1)/((10^B O2)-1) non-dimensional unit related
to vacuum tower packing
depth and inlet/outlet
CO2 levels
Outlet N2 Stage 2 = Outlet N2 Stage 1*(8*(HO2*Xe2/HN2)*((10^BN2)-1)+7*(Outlet O2 Stage 2 - Outlet O2 Stage 1 ))/(8*(HO2*Xe2/HN2)*((10^BN2)-1)+7*(10^BN2)*(Outlet O2 Stage 2 - Outlet O2 Stage 1)) (ppm)
O2 Flow Rate = (Flow Rate/4.4029)*(Outlet O2 Stage 1 - Outlet O2 Stage 2)/32 (mol/hr)
N2 Flow Rate = (Flow Rate/4.4029)*(Outlet N2 Stage 1 - Outlet N2 Stage 2)/28 (mol/hr)
CO2 Flow Rate = (Flow Rate/4.4029)*((Inlet CO2^0.5)-1)/44 (mol/hr)
O2 Moles = O2 Flow Rate / (O2 Flow Rate + N2 Flow Rate + CO2 Flow Rate)
Pressure Air = H O2 * Xe2/ O2 Moles (mm Hg) pressure (a function of vacuum tower packing depth)
Pressure Total = Pressure H2O + Pressure Air (mm Hg) pressure (a function of vacuum tower packing depth)
Gas mass flow rate air = Flow Rate O2 + Flow Rate N2 + Flow Rate CO2 (mol/hr) quantity of gas (PV value), a mass flow rate
Vessel Sizing:
Degasifier Tower Sizing
Tower straight side height = (packing depth stage1*12.0) + (inlet to packing height*12.0) + inlet to upperhead TL+ (packing depth stage2*12.0) + (loop seal height*12.0) (inch)
Degasifier Tower Sizing
Clearwell Volume Required = Flow Rate*Clearwell Retention Time (gal)
Clearwell Volume Calculated = 3.14159*(clearwell diameter/24.0)**2*((clearwell straight side height - (lll alarm + ll alarm + hl alarm + inlet diameter + overflow diameter)-((flange diameter – pump outlet diameter)/2))/12)*7.5 (gal)
Clearwell Volume Calculated must be always greater than Clearwell Volume Required
Straight side height excluding skirt = Tower Straight Height + Clearwell Straight Height (inch)
Straight side height Including skirt = Tower Straight Height + Clearwell Straight + Skirt Straight Height (inch)
