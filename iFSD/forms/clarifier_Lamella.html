Calculations

Number of streams required to execute the unitop
Input- 1 Stream
Output - 2 Stream


Clarifier Blowdown Concentration = Clarifier Sludge Blowdown / 100.0*1000000.0 ppm
C.F. = clarifier_blowdown_concentration/Feed TSS
Turbidity = Feed TSS /2.0
Chemical Dosage
Al2(SO4)3 = (color*0.5)+(turbidity*0.025) ppm
FeCl3 = ((color*0.5)+(turbidity*0.025))*0.42 ppm
PAC = (color*0.5)+(turbidity*0.025))*0.42 ppm
Cationic Polymer = ((color*0.5)+(turbidity*0.025))*0.333 ppm
Sludge Generation
Total Incoming Solids = Feed TSS + (Chemical Dosage*Kg dry sludge/kg coagulant) ppm
Clarified Water Solids Load (100% Dry Solids) = Clarifier Product Flow Rate* Total Incoming Solids*24/1000 kg/day
Blowdown Solids Load (100% Dry Solids) = Clarifier Blowdown Flow Rate* Total Incoming Solids*24/1000 kg/day
Total Incoming Water Solids Load (100% Dry Solids) = Clarified Water Solids Load + Blowdown Solids Load kg/day
Adjusted Clarifier Blowdown TSS
Solid Contact Clarifier Design
Hydraulic Surface Loading Rate-Based on Product Flow Rate = Clarifier Product Flow Rate/Clarification Zone Surface Area (gpm/ft2)
Hydraulic Surface Loading Rate-Based on Feed Flow Rate = Clarifier FeedFlow Rate /Clarification Zone Surface Area (gpm/ft2)
