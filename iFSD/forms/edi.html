Please add Note:

EDI module is built on basis of E-cell Process Calculation Spreadsheet (E-Calc 4.0.5d)

  • The composition of product stream is fixed at the max predicted ion compositions for given resistivity. The predictions are based on guaranteed product water resistivity.
  • Waste stream flow is calculated as sum of concentrate bleed and electrode bleed streams.

Input Parameter Ranges

  1. Target Resistivity : 1-16 Mohm.cm for E cell, MK-3, MK-3 MiniHT Industrial and 1-10 Mohm.cm for MK-3 PharmaHT, MK-3 MiniHT Pharmaceutical.
  2. Flow per Stack: 10.0 - 28.0 gpm or 2.3 -6.4 m3/hr for E cell;7.5 - 20.0 gpm or 1.7 -4.5 m3/hr for MK-3 and MK-3 PharmaHT;2.5 - 6.7 gpm or 0.567 - 1.5 m3/hr for MK-3 MiniHT Pharmaceutical;2-6 gpm or 0.45-1.36 m3/hr for MK-3 MiniHT Industrial. On top of this, flow per stack should not exceed product flow value.
  3. Temperature: 41-100 deg F or 5-38 deg C
  4. Product Flow:>10 gpm or >2.27 m3/hr for E cell;>7.5 gpm or >1.7 m3/hr for MK-3,MK-3 PharmaHT; >2.5 gpm or >0.567 m3/hr for MK-3 MiniHT Pharmaceutical, 2-6 gpm or 0.45-1.36 m3/hr for MK-3 MiniHT Industrial.
  5. Rectifier Efficiency :40%-95%
  6. Feed Pressure: If co current is selected(45-100psi or 3.1 - 6.9 bar for E cell, MK-3,MK-3 PharmaHT,MK-3 MiniHT Pharmaceutical,MK-3 MiniHT Industrial.)If co current is not selected(60-100psi or 4.1 - 6.9 bar for E cell, MK-3,MK-3 PharmaHT,MK-3 MiniHT Pharmaceutical,MK-3 MiniHT Industrial.)
