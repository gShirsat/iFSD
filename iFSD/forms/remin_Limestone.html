Calculations

Number of streams required to execute the unitop

  • Water composition on addition of chemicals
  • CaCO3 = Stream 2 Ca as CaCO3 - Stream 3 Ca as CaCO3
  • CO2 Consumption Stream 1a = Flow Rate stream 1a* CO2 stream 1a/1000
  • CO2 Consumption Stream 1b = Flow Rate stream 1b* CO2 stream 1b/1000
  • CaCO3 Consumption = CaCO3*Flow Rate stream 3/1000
  • H2SO4 Consumption = Flow Rate * H2SO4/1000
  • NaOH Consumption = Flow Rate * NaOH/1000

Gravity Remineralization Design

Flowrate through the Remin System: From Remin- Calc sheet (m3/h)
Reactor Area for one Unit = Reactor Length * Reactor Width (m2)
Based on velocity Max flowrate/Unit = Round(Reactor Area for one Unit * Contact Time) (m3/h)
Total Number of Units required = Roundup(Flowrate through the Remin System / Based on velocity Max flowrate/Unit)
Total Number of units provided = Total Number of Units required + Number of Spare Units
Volume based on Minimum bed depth = Reactor Area for one Unit / Minimum Bed depth (m3)
Velocity and Contact Time Check (Velocity <= 10 m/hr and Contact Time >= 7 min)
Velocity = Flowrate through the Remin System /Reactor Area for one Unit (m/hr)
Contact Time = Volume based on Minimum bed depth*60 / Flowrate through the Remin System (min)
CaCO3 Volume per Reactor for full bed depth = Reactor Area for one Unit*Bed depth assumed /1000 (m3)
CaCO3 Volume per Reactor for min bed depth = Reactor Area for one Unit*Min Bed depth assumed /1000 (m3)
Usable Volume = Reactor Area for one Unit*(Assumed Bed depth*Reduction in Finished bed depth -Minimum Bed depth)/1000 (m3)
CaCO3 Consumption/Reactor@99% = Flowrate*Specific CaCO3 consumption/1000*24/Assumed CaCO3 specific gravity / Assumed CaCO3 purity (m3/day)
# of Days Before filling = Usable Volume / CaCO3 Consumption/Reactor@99%
Volume for each Reactor = Reactor Area for one Unit*(Assumed Bed depth â Minimum Bed depth)/1000 (m3)
Treated Water Volume /Reactor = Flowrate* 24*# of Days Before filling (m3/fill)

Pressure Remineralization Design

Design Guidelines:

Space Velocity 40 (m/hr)
Contact Time 6 (min)
Cleaning Flowrate (30-35 m/h 2BV) 35 (m/hr)
Duration of Cleaning flow at 35m/h 5 (min)
Bed depth consumed before refill 10 (%)

Calculations:

Flowrate through the Remin System: From Remin- Calc sheet (m3/h)
Reactor Area for one Unit = pi/4*Vessel Diameter^2* (m2)
Based on min contact time flowrate/Unit = Round(Reactor Area for one Unit / Contact Time*60) (m3/h)
Total Number of Units required = Roundup(Flowrate through the Remin System / Based on min contact time flowrate/Unit )
Total Number of units provided = Total Number of Units required + Number of Spare Units
CaCO3 Volume per Reactor for full bed depth = Assumed Total Bed depth* Reactor Area for one Unit/1000 (m3)
Refill Volume = CaCO3 Volume per Reactor for full bed depth* Bed depth consumed before refill (m3)
Volume based on total bed depth = Assumed Total Bed depth* Reactor Area for one Unit/1000 (m3)
Volume based on Minimum bed depth = Volume based on total bed depth - Refill Volume (m3)
Velocity and Contact Time Check (Velocity <= 40 m/hr and Contact Time >= 6 min)
Velocity = Flowrate through the Remin System /Reactor Area for one Unit (m/hr)
Contact Time = Volume based on Minimum bed depth*60 / Flowrate through the Remin System (min)
CaCO3 Consumption/Reactor@99% = Flowrate*Specific CaCO3 consumption/1000*24/Assumed CaCO3 specific gravity / Assumed CaCO3 purity (m3/day)
# of Days Before filling = Refill Volume / CaCO3 Consumption/Reactor@99%
Volume for each Reactor = Refill Volume (m3)
Treated Water Volume /Reactor = Flowrate* 24*# of Days Before filling (m3/fill)
